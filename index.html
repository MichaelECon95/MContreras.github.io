Know Me

Originally from Austin, Texas, I started my journey in customer service before proudly serving in the United States Air Force Reserves. There, I honed vital skills in logistics, strategic thinking, and organization. Balancing military service and everyday life, I learned the importance of effective communication and time management.

Outside my work, I find balance and energy in weight lifting and jump-rope. When not engaged in these activities, I cherish moments playing with my dog in the park or diving into a captivating book.

During my eight-year military service, I expanded my horizons by joining Apple as a Mac Expert. Here, I assisted customers with tech solutions, trained new team members, and pioneered strategies in project management. This journey concluded when I chose to accept a deployment opportunity for the military.

Post-deployment, I pursued higher education in San Antonio, Texas. Concurrently, I furthered my career at H-E-B, mastering the logistics of trailer and product operations along with fostering a safe and efficient work environment. My role involved guiding team members, ensuring adherence to set guidelines, and emphasizing vital OSHA standards.

Amid the Covid era, I earned an associate degree in finance, complementing my hands-on experience at H-E-B. Drawing from this wealth of knowledge, I embarked on a new challenge at Metrica Inc. as an Accounting Specialist. Here, I manage financial transactions from global clientele, while ensuring precision and integrity in our financial data.

Reflecting on my diverse experiences, I recognized my passion for data-driven insights that drive business growth and efficiency. With this in mind, I'm actively pursuing a Master's in Business Analytics at The University of Texas San Antonio. I believe in life long learning and I eagerly anticipate forging new connections, collaborating with peers, and embarking on more enriching adventures.
